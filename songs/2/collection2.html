music collection 1: november sixth 2020 to december thirtieth 2020

mister x

this was supposed fit the theme of a detective working in the shadows, and i think i captured that pretty well.

trivia:

the title, "Mister X" comes from the game "Scotland Yard" where one player is "Mr. X" or "Ms. X" and the other players have to catch them. It's a really fun game, so if you have a friend group, i would recommend giving it a try.

chilled out sunrise

chill piece i made during winter break off of school. I tried to fuse a ton of independent instruments from garageband into one track, which sounds pretty questionable. (erhu, koto, guzheng, 808 bass, 808 flex, and the chord synths which i can't remember the name of)

the counting sample was recorded by me, of myself whispering.

the microwave sample was recorded by me, of my microwave macaroni and cheese.

sword

attempt at a techno-style piece. The instrumentation in this one is really weird, from using flutes and piano to guitar and bassoon. I kind of like how it turned out, but you can judge for yourself.

absence of color

This is a track i made, inspired by littlebigplanet's gradually building music. It sounds kinda cacophonous towards the end, but it can be looped in other areas. I personally don't really like this track, but it's a different style than what i usually do.

drum experiment 7

this was the seventh entry in a series of "drum experiments" i did which I decided to make into a full song. The main loop in this is from garageband, called "Lost In Thought Piano 02" in the keyboard collection sound pack. The rest is made by me. I mostly worked on this at lunch at school

shield

sequel to sword, which i don't like very much because of the fact that this was somewhat rushed
