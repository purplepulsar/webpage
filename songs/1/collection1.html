(not) my final song

fourth and presumably final entry in the "bounce" series. It was originally going to be my last song, because my parents were restricting my access to my ipad (which i use for music production). Well, that obviously got sorted out and i kept making music. However, unless i feel like i want to, i will probably not make music in this instrumentation/style for a while (i also ran out of catchy leitmotifs for these pieces).

trivia: the choppy voice samples (recorded by me, of course, otherwise i couldn't upload this to newgrounds) were (clearly) inspired by "Brain Power" by NOMA. the garbled voice at the beginning (also my voice, put through the robot filter in garageband) says a bunch of things i can't remember, one of them being "adhd" (which i have) and another being "paradox" which i don't really know why i put in.
