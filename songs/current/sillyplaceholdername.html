aquarium park remix

aural (by ear) recreation/remix of aquarium park from sonic colors. recreating the drumloop alone took up about twenty audio channels on garageband (i can't just create a drum rack) and was very time consuming.
now, i know that people had found the original drumloop for aquarium park, but i wanted to make it my own. also, i didn't realize this at the time, but the classical grand sound in garageband is the exact same as the original in the song.
